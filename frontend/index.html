HOW IT WORKS
01
Deposit

Send MOTO or any OP-20 token into the vault. Your funds stay on-chain, non-custodial, under your control.

02
Schedule

Set interval (in blocks) and swap size. 6 blocks ≈ 1 hour. 144 blocks ≈ 1 day. You choose the cadence.

03
Auto-Swap

Each interval, the contract routes through Motoswap to buy your target token at market price. Trustless.

04
Accumulate

Watch your stack grow. Withdraw at any time. Cancel anytime. Your keys, your sats, Bitcoin L1.
